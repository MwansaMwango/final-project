Motivation

CCTV is often the goto solution for employee theft. Business owners can't be present at all times, ot lack resources to watch hours of video footage detect fraud. Employee theft continues to hinder profit margins for business owners, especially in most developing countries were average annual income is under US$ 3,000! (World Data, 2019)

Internal Theft

40% of Australian companies are uncovering internal fraud and according to Price Waterhouse Coopers detection rates are higher in Australia than the rest of the world.

An estimate from The American Society of Employers is that 20% of every dollar earned by a U.S. company is lost to employee theft.

According to The U.S. Chamber of Commerce 75% of employees steal from the workplace and most do so repeatedly. Small businesses with less than 100 employees had the highest median loss at $150,000. (Association of Certified Fraud Examiners)

Only 16% of companies call the police to launch an employee theft investigation. (National Federation of Independent Business)
