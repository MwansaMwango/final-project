Current Situation

CCTV has been the goto solution to prevent employee or internal theft. Business managers can't be present at all times and are too busy to review hours of video footage. Employee theft continues to hinder profit margins for business owners, especially in most developing countries with average annual income is under US$ 3,000! (World Data, 2019)

How bad is internal theft?

40% of Australian companies are uncovering internal fraud and according to Price Waterhouse Coopers detection rates are higher in Australia than the rest of the world.
An estimate from The American Society of Employers is that 20% of every dollar earned by a U.S. company is lost to employee theft.
According to The U.S. Chamber of Commerce 75% of employees steal from the workplace and most do so repeatedly. Small businesses with less than 100 employees had the highest median loss at $150,000. (Association of Certified Fraud Examiners)
Only 16% of companies call the police to launch an employee theft investigation. (National Federation of Independent Business)

Solution

BizzWatch is a solution that leverages computer vision and data analytics to detect, audit and predict revenue or customer demand.

Fraud Detection

Object detection is used to count people or products and compared to ground-truth or revenue, consumption, etc.

Sales/Customer Prediction

If we can count number of people or products over time, we can predict sales and understand influencing factors form external variables or features.

Resource Optimisation

Understanding the peaks and troughs of demand behaviour allows optimised resource allocation of staff, goods and services to better serve the clients.

Minimum Viable Product (MVP) WebApp Functionality

The ability to detect objects accurately and fast is key to an effective product. Thereafter, aggregation and correlation with other features for prediction can be deployed.
